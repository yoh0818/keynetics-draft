Famous Motion Graphics Companies Reference

Ordinary Folk

Ordinary Folk is a motion graphics studio based in Vancouver, Canada. They are known for leading trends in 2D motion graphics.

Super Very More

Super Very More is a motion graphics studio based in Seoul, South Korea. They specialize in creating 3D motion graphics.

The Furrow

The Furrow is a motion graphics studio based in Lexington, Kentucky, USA. They are known for bold, trendy visuals and striking use of colour.

FlatWhite Motion

FlatWhite Motion is a motion graphics studio based in Sydney and Qingdao. They create motion graphics using character animation and hand-drawn techniques.
