Famous Motion Graphics Artists Reference

Seven

Seven is a motion graphics designer based in Korea and a pioneer of the faux 3D trend. He typically creates motion graphics works based on faux 3D and minimalist art.

Ben Marriott

Ben Marriott is a motion graphics designer based in Sydney and an artist who creates motion graphics using unique texturing techniques.

DeeKay

Deekay is a motion graphics designer and animator based in the United States, and he usually creates motion graphics featuring characters.

Mabelous

Mabelous is a motion graphics designer based in Korea who creates motion graphics by skillfully blending 2D and 3D elements.
